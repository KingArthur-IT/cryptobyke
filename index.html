We decided to create a custom collection of metaverse characters on our bikes

When buying an nft collection, you will have a 20% discount on the purchase of a bike if you buy through the Binance or Opensea platforms. If you did not have time to purchase nft, then you will be given the opportunity to buy on our Market Place, after which you will have a 15% discount on the next bike purchase. For fans of passive earning, we add Staking. You can earn from 2.5 to 10% additional income by becoming the first investors in the project.

Our NFTs will be divided into groups

1000 meta characters

Stage 1

We will publish the first 300 NFTs on the Binance platform. All funds from the sold collection will go to the further liquidity of our token. Subsequent sales will take place on our marketplace, where you will receive a bonus of 15% of our token from the NFT purchase amount. All of our NFTs will have a hashrate to mine the game token.

2000 meta characters

Stage 2

In the future, after the launch of the game, we will start developing AR reality, which will allow you to bring your NFTs to life. Also during this period, you will get the opportunity to create your own metaverse character and bring it to life on our bike.

3000 meta characters

Stage 3

The first draw will be 1000 NFT, all funds will go to the liquidity of the token. After the release of Metabike coin, we will release another 1000 NFTs, but with other surprises for you. The total circulation of our collection will be 5000 NFTs.
