What kind
of game is it?

What makes it special is that we are the first game in the world that gives you the opportunity to bring your bikes and NFTs to life.You can also create your own motorcycle, improve it to your taste, place it on our marketplace for further sale or exchange with other users. And also, every half a year we will improve the game and its functionality, only for the better.

How to start playing!

The more powerful the bike – the more power you have to mine

The more bikes you have in your collection – the more of our coin output you have

Mining

Our bike building technology allows you to mine our token before it even launches on the platform and use the in-game currency in the future internally, to buy NFT bikes and other items. How much you mine in the beginning is how much you get when the coin comes out
